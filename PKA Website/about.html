About Precious Kiddies Academy

Precious Kiddies Academy, founded in 2013, began as a humble endeavor to provide quality education and holistic development for young children. The founders, Mr. and Mrs. Adebiyi, started this institution with a passion for nurturing young minds, intending to create a “home away from home” for every child. What began with just ten students has flourished over the years, growing into a thriving academic community with over 150 students across preparatory, nursery, and primary school sections.

Precious Kiddies Academy stands today as a beacon of excellence in early childhood education, dedicated to fostering academic, moral, and social growth for all students. A Strong Foundation in Academic Excellence and Holistic Development. From its inception, Precious Kiddies Academy has been committed to providing an educational experience that goes beyond mere academic achievement. With a comprehensive and well-structured curriculum, the academy covers essential areas such as language, mathematics, science, social studies, and physical education. This approach aims to foster intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills.

Additionally, special attention is given to moral education, personal growth, and emotional intelligence, with teachers and caregivers guiding students to develop kindness, empathy, and respect for themselves and others.

Award-Winning Culture: Achievements in Competitions and Beyond Precious Kiddies Academy has garnered numerous awards over the years, marking its place as a top competitor in various local and regional academic events. Our students have consistently excelled in spelling bees, quiz competitions, Qur'an recitation contests, and even inter-school sports tournaments. Each victory not only showcases the students' hard work and abilities but also highlights the dedication of our staff in nurturing and motivating our young learners. The school’s participation in these competitions goes beyond the pursuit of awards. We see them as valuable opportunities for our students to build confidence, enhance public speaking skills, and develop a healthy competitive spirit. Students return from these events more resilient, with a sense of pride and accomplishment that reflects positively in their classroom engagement.

A Conducive Learning Environment: Safe, Stimulating, and Supportive A child’s environment greatly impacts their willingness and enthusiasm to learn, and at Precious Kiddies Academy, we understand this deeply. Our school’s infrastructure has been thoughtfully designed to create a warm and safe space where students feel at home. Classrooms are bright, spacious, and equipped with age-appropriate learning materials. Interactive boards, story corners, and colorful decorations make the learning space both enjoyable and engaging.

Dedicated and Experienced Tutors: Our educators at Precious Kiddies Academy are more than just teachers; they are mentors, guides, and role models who play an instrumental role in our students’ lives. With years of experience and specialized training in early childhood education, they bring warmth, creativity, and passion to the classroom. Each tutor is dedicated to identifying and nurturing the unique strengths of every child, encouraging them to explore, ask questions, and grow at their own pace.

Extracurricular Opportunities: Encouraging Talents Beyond the Classroom At Precious Kiddies Academy, we understand that learning happens both within and beyond the classroom. That’s why we provide a diverse range of extracurricular activities, including , art, and sports. These activities allow students to explore their interests, develop new skills, and express themselves creatively. For students with a keen interest in sports, our physical education programs include training and participation in inter-school sports competitions. We believe that such experiences are vital for building confidence, teamwork, and leadership skills.

In conclusion, Precious Kiddies Academy is more than just a school; it is a nurturing community that values each child’s individuality. We are proud of our journey from a small establishment with ten students to a thriving institution of over 150 children. Our success is a testament to the hard work, dedication, and shared vision of our founders, teachers, parents, and students. We look forward to many more years of growth, achievement, and impact, as we continue to provide an outstanding educational experience that truly makes a difference.
