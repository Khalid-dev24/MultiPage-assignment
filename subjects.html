Subjects Offered

At Precious Kiddies Academy, we offer a well-rounded curriculum that aims to nurture both the academic and personal growth of each child. Our subjects are tailored to meet the learning needs of students in the Preparatory, Nursery, and Primary sections, preparing them for a lifetime of success.

Core Subjects
Mathematics – Building strong numerical and problem-solving skills.

English Language – Focusing on reading, writing, grammar, and communication skills.

Science – Introducing fundamental scientific concepts and hands-on learning.

Social Studies – Teaching history, geography, and cultural awareness.

Additional Subjects
Basic Technology – Developing early technology and digital skills.

Creative Arts – Encouraging creativity through drawing, painting, and crafts.

Physical & Health Education – Promoting physical fitness and healthy lifestyle choices.

Religious & Moral Education – Nurturing values, ethics, and cultural understanding.

Civic Education – Instilling civic responsibility and community awareness.

Each subject is designed to engage young learners and build a strong foundation for future studies. We continually update our curriculum to include interactive and innovative teaching approaches. For more information on our curriculum or to discuss your child’s specific learning needs, please contact our school administration.
