Events

At Precious Kiddies Academy, we organize a variety of exciting events each year to enrich our students’ learning experiences, encourage creativity, and build strong community bonds. Here’s a look at some of our key annual events:

1. Cultural Day: A vibrant celebration of diversity, where students dress in traditional attire, perform cultural dances, and learn about different heritages. This day fosters respect and appreciation for cultural diversity.

2. Color Day: Students and staff dress in the color theme of the day, bringing fun and creativity into the school environment. Activities are centered around color recognition, art, and learning through visual experiences.

3. Sports Day: A day dedicated to physical activities and team spirit! Students participate in a range of sports, track events, and games, promoting fitness, teamwork, and sportsmanship.

4. Fruits Day: A fun and educational event where students explore the importance of healthy eating. They learn about various fruits, sample them, and even create fruit-based art, fostering healthy eating habits from a young age.

5. Educational Excursions: Each term, students enjoy trips to interesting locations like museums, science centers, and parks. These excursions provide hands-on learning experiences and bring classroom lessons to life.

6. Graduation & Awards Ceremony: At the end of the academic year, we celebrate our students' achievements with a formal graduation and awards day. Outstanding academic and extracurricular performances are recognized, and we bid farewell to our graduating students.

These events are an integral part of our school community, designed to inspire joy, learning, and a love for exploration in every student. Parents and guardians are always encouraged to join in and celebrate with us!
