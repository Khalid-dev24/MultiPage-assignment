Admission Process

Welcome to Precious Kiddies Academy! Our admission process is designed to make joining our school easy and welcoming for all families.

1. Campus Visit & Inquiry: Schedule a visit to our campus to tour our facilities and discuss any questions with our staff.

2. Application Form Collect the application form from our school office or download it online. Fill in the details and submit it with the required documents.

3. Entrance Assessment To help place your child in the appropriate class, we conduct a short assessment to understand their current academic level.
